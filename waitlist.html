What early access is

AetherForge is an independent, early-stage project, and the product is still pre-launch. Early access means we invite people from the waitlist to try features as they stabilize — backtesting, the signal dashboard, risk reports, and data tools — and to share feedback that shapes what we build next. Early access is free.

How to join

There is no automated signup form yet. To join the waitlist:

  • Email contact@aetherforge.bond
  • Use the subject line “AetherForge waitlist”
  • Optionally, tell us a little about what you trade or research — it helps us prioritise invites

If your mail app doesn't open when you click the button above, just copy the address contact@aetherforge.bond into whatever email you normally use.

What happens after you email

We're a small, early-stage project, so this is handled by a human — not an automated system. We read your email, add you to the list, and reach out when a spot opens. We can't promise a specific date or guarantee access, and we'll be honest with you about timing.

Your data & no spam

We use your email only to contact you about early access. We don't sell it and we don't send marketing blasts. You can reply to any message to be removed at any time. See our Privacy Policy for details.

AetherForge provides research and educational tools only — not financial, investment, or trading advice, and it never executes trades or holds funds. Please read our Risk Disclaimer.
