What it does

Everything you need to test an idea

Five building blocks for evidence-based crypto research. AetherForge gives you the tools — you keep the decisions.

Backtesting

Test strategies against historical market & on-chain data with transparent fees, slippage, and assumptions. Every report shows its methodology and its limitations.

Signal monitoring

Track the indicators and strategy states you care about on a dashboard, with alerts when they change. Informational only — you decide and execute yourself.

Risk reports

Drawdown, volatility, exposure, and scenario breakdowns — so you understand the downside before you ever think about the upside.

Data pipelines

Curated, reproducible market and on-chain data feeding research and backtests — so results are consistent and re-runnable.

Strategy research subscriptions

Follow research strategies and get notified on state changes. No auto-trading, no custody — we never touch your funds or exchange keys.
