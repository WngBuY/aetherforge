About

From prototype to production agents

AetherForge is an AI agent platform that helps developers and teams move agents out of notebooks and into the real world. Compose agents across multiple foundation models, connect them to your data and tools, and run them reliably at scale — all from a unified environment. Our mission is to make autonomous, multi-model agents practical for every team.

Multi-model by design

Route each step to the model best suited for the job. Mix providers freely and avoid lock-in to a single vendor.

Tools & data connectors

Give agents access to APIs, databases, and live data streams so they can act on real, current information.

Observability & control

Trace every run, evaluate outputs, and enforce guardrails — so you can ship agents you actually trust.
